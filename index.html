What is Quality Education & Its Importance

Quality Education

  • Quality Education goes beyond literacy and numeracy
  • It ensures inclusive, equitable and lifelong learning opportunities
  • Focus on skills:
    • Critical thinking
    • Creativity
    • Problem-solving
    • Empathy
    • Digital Literacy

Its Importance

Empowerment

Reduces poverty & inequality

Strengthens Communities

Promotes gender equality & peace

Boosts Economy

Skilled workforce for future jobs

Sustainability

Supports all other SDGs (health, climate, innovation)

How to Achieve Quality Education

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the World." — Nelson Mandela
  • Invest in Infrastructure & Resources
  • Teacher training to rebuild student-centric, innovative methods of teaching in them
  • Affordable access to internet and devices to encourage underprivileged for quality education
  • Collaboration: GOVERNMENT + NGOs + PRIVATE SECTORS + COMMUNITIES to provide scholarships for classifying Scholars
